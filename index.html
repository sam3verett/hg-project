Panem's Predatory Propaganda

This project seeks to analyze the various rhetorical strategies used by the Capitol of Panem (and other organizations like the rebel groups) over the course of the Hunger Games franchise, seen in all of the books, movies, and outside lore.

One of the key ways the Capitol maintains such a strong hold over each of the districts is via propaganda strategies used to reinforce the idea that they are indomitable and all-powerful, and anyone else is practically subhuman in comparison. This is seen in televised broadcasts, the Games themselves, and much more.
